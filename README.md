# Video-Games-Sales
Video Game Sales Analytics Dashboard


1. Short Description / Purpose:
- An interactive sales analytics dashboard developed to analyze global video game sales performance across platforms, genres, publishers, and regions. The dashboard helps identify top-performing segments and sales  trends to support data-driven business decisions.


2. Tech Stack:
- Power BI (Dashboard Development & Data Visualization)
- DAX (Calculated Measures & KPIs)
- Excel / CSV (Data Handling & Transformation)


3.Data Source:
- The dataset was obtained from Kaggle and contains historical video game sales data including title, platform, genre, publisher, release year, and regional/global sales figures.


4.Features / Highlights:

1. Business Problem:
- Gaming companies and publishers need clear insights into sales performance across platforms, genres, and regions. Without structured analytics, it becomes difficult to identify profitable segments and optimize release strategies.

2. Goal of the Dashboard / Project:
- To analyze global and regional video game sales trends.
- To identify top-performing platforms, genres, and publishers.
- To provide actionable insights for strategic decision-making in the gaming industry.

3. Walkthrough of Key Visuals:
- KPI Cards showing total global sales, top-selling genre, and leading platform.
- Bar chart displaying sales by genre and platform.
- Line chart visualizing sales trends over different years.
- Stacked column chart comparing regional sales (NA, EU, JP, Others).
- Top 10 games table based on global sales.
- Interactive filters for year, genre, platform, and publisher.

4. Business Impact and Insights:
- Identified the most profitable genres and platforms.
- Highlighted regional market preferences and demand patterns.
- Revealed peak sales periods and long-term industry trends.
- Supported strategic planning for product launches and market targeting.
